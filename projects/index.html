Projects

Any technology-based knowledge needs to be both theoretical as well as practical, and Technoforum is one such platform where they focus on increasing the knowledge and skills of any student with any technology they want by project-based learning. Project-based learning is one of the most appreciated ways to learn and explore new technologies. Technoforum believes in project-based learning and encourages the student to explore different technologies by providing a platform to showcase their projects. It not only helps the student gain knowledge about a particular technology but builds up self-confidence, critical thinking skills, and project management skills.
