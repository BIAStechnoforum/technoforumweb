    Learning from the same individual in the same style often gets boring. It's believed knowledge is best learned when things are shaken. Webinars are one of the ways to do so. Often we are restricted to the knowledge by the limited resources but webinars help fill the void by providing interactions with teachers and experts in those fields.     Coding is one of the things that is easy to do. Even if you get stuck you can google the code for a solution. There is no fun in that.     Building your own IoT devices, robots, and other electronic-based products are good. It is in the real-world that the strength of the product is tested and what other places to know the drawbacks than a competition of such unique ideas and products. Electromania is one such competition where you can bring your robots for events such as robowar, roborace, etc. You can also bring in your IoT devices to showcase how the device you created is helpful in today's world.
