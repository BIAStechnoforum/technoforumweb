Technoforum Webinars

Learning from the same individual in the same style often gets boring. It's believed knowledge is best learned when things are shaken. Webinars are one of the ways to do so. Interacting with teachers and students from other colleges let you explore how the one you are going to compete against are being trained. Often we are restricted to the knowledge by the limited resources but webinars help fill the void by providing interactions with teachers and experts in those fields. Classroom teaching is often restricted, due to the completion of the syllabus on time. Webinars help fill this void by specifying a time to teach on those technologies.
